500 px | Portrait of Cities : Boston vs Hefei

Xingyue Li | IDV program | NEU

This is a project comparing two cities, Boston and Hefei, based on photos uploaded by users on 500px.com. Because 500px provides a clear and accesible API reference to developers, I choose 150 popular images from each city to show the differences of cities.
